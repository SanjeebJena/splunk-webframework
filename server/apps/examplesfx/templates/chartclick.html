Chart Clicks

Many pages display information using charts, whether they are pie charts, bar charts, or other types. These charts can be very useful for displaying summary information, and we want to allow users to drill down and get more fine-grained detail.

This example shows how one can allow in-page detail drilldown on a chart. When we click on one wedge of the pie chart, we see the single-value view on its right get populated with this information. In this case we are simply powering another search, but any action can be taken inside the click handler for the chart.
